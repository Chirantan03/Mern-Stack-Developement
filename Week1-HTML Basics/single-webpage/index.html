Biography

So far, Joshua Kimmich has played 74 international matches for Germany. He scored a total of five goals in these games. He claimed the Confederations Cup with Germany in 2017. He won the Champions League while playing for FC Bayern München in 2019/20. He won the DFB-Pokal in 2015/16, 2018/19 and 2019/20 with FC Bayern München. Joshua Kimmich won the Supercup in 2016, 2017, 2018, 2020, 2021 and 2022 with FC Bayern München. With a total of seven trophies up to this point in his career, he has won the 13th-most titles in the German championship.
